Rocket Leauge

Next up, Rocket Leauge. Same Internet, Specs and all that. It preformed Great, Exept for the trearing... You see, I have an Anti-Tear Monitor, I figured out though, that I needed to turn on V-Sync. THANK THE GODS OF V-SYNC, V-Sync or Vertical sync will do something.. Let google explain it. "VSync, or vertical sync, is a graphics technology that synchronizes the frame rate of a game and the refresh rate of a gaming monitor. First developed by GPU manufacturers, this tech was a way to deal with screen tearing, which is when your screen displays portions of multiple frames in one go." Thank you google. So screen tearing WAS a problem but is not anymore. Aside from that, Rocket Leauge Preforms really well. Smooth and not much freezing, if any. I Love playing the game and now that I have more time to play games it's in 2nd place of games I play most with this extra time on my hands.
